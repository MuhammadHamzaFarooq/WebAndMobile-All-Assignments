Products & Prices

Product and Price

There are no hidden charges when you order from Easy online store. All cost are 100% visible on the checkout page which includes Product Price + Shipment Charges.

Prices on Easy online store are not negotiable. Easy online store has thousands of sellers which offer you the best prices and deals.

Some of our products are offered by different sellers which allows you to chose a vendor and price according to your requirements.

Order

Order Cancellation

If you wish to cancel your order before it is shipped, please call (0317)-4750799 speak to a Customer Service rep.

If your order is already on its way to you or if you have already received your order, you will not be able to cancel the order but you may be able to return your product.
